About Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan, is a modern planned city known for its clean environment, well-structured layout, and vibrant cultural scene. The city features numerous parks, museums, and government buildings that showcase the nation's history and achievements. Visitors can explore the Pakistan Monument, Faisal Mosque, and the National Museum to learn about the country's rich heritage. With its blend of modern amenities and natural beauty, Islamabad offers a unique and comfortable experience for travelers.
